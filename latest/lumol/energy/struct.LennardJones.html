Lennard-Jones potential.

The following expression of Lennard-Jones potential is used: V(r) = 4 * epsilon * ((sigma/r)^12 - (sigma/r)^6) where sigma is the Lennard-Jones distance constant, and epsilon the Lennard-Jones energetic constant.

Examples

use lumol::energy::Potential;
use lumol::energy::LennardJones;

let potential = LennardJones{sigma: 2.0, epsilon: 10.0};
assert_eq!(potential.energy(2.0), 0.0);
assert_eq!(potential.energy(3.0), -3.203365942785746);

assert_eq!(potential.force(2.0), 120.0);Run
